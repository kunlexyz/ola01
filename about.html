About The Author
The last born in a family of four, Olusanya Emmanuel Oladehinde (Sampress) was born in Awoyokun, Mushin Local Government Area of Lagos State.
He attended Shyllon Primary School. He furthers his education career by obtaining SSCE at Homat Pivotal Secondary School. His currently in Lagos Polytechnic in (Yaba College of Technology) studying Mass Communication.
His also a Music-Partner with a well-recognized Band known as T-Jazz Concept. He has written some dynamic and interesting short stories for some Private Schools. This is his first book to be published.
